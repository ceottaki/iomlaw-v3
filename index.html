Welcome to BridsonHalsall.
An international law practice in the Isle of Man.

Founded in 2006, by Andy Bridson and Richard Halsall, our practice is highly experienced and advises on a commensurately broad range of commercial and non-commercial matters for private clients and organisations.

While dealing with all aspects of Isle of Man law, BridsonHalsall acts for international clients and we have excellent connections to a range of specialists in other jurisdictions who can assist with specific expertise as required.

We regularly liaise with foreign lawyers in multi-jurisdictional matters and engage trusted consultants on specialised work.

Today, BridsonHalsall has expanded significantly and is one of the Isle of Man’s leading practices.

We pride ourselves on our ability to combine incisive analysis of legal issues with sound commercial judgment and a practical, friendly approach for our clients.

In the Isle of Man legal profession, advocates undertake the role of both solicitor and barrister.
Commercial litigation

Focused expertise with significant experience in commercial and trust disputes or major negligence cases, including professional negligence and fraud.

Employment

We offer a comprehensive understanding of employment issues, both for HR departments, employers and employees.

Immigration

We provide unrivalled expertise in the Isle of Man’s immigration legislation and rules with particular specialisms in high net worth individuals and international businesses.

Family law

A sensitive and caring approach to difficult family legal matters.

Personal litigation

An effective and supportive approach to personal disputes and personal injury.

Compliance and Crisis response

With complex regulations and severe consequences for non-compliance, we offer outstanding Prevention and ‘First Call’ Crisis services.

Crime

Whether corporate crime, such as fraud, money laundering a health and safety failure, or general crime from road traffic matters to homicide, we offer astute and pragmatic advocacy.

Property

We act in all aspects of Commercial and Residential Property on both Freehold and Leasehold.
